Install App

Install this app on your device for quick access. It works offline after the first visit — no app store needed.

Android (Chrome)
Tap the three dots menu (top right) → "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
iPhone / iPad (Safari only)
Tap the Share button (square with arrow) → scroll down → "Add to Home Screen"
Desktop (Chrome / Edge)
Look for the install icon in the address bar (right side) → click "Install"
Once installed, the app opens in its own window without a browser bar and works completely offline. Your data stays on your device.

How to Transfer Between Devices

  1. 1
    On Device A: Click "Full Backup (JSON)" — this saves a file to your device
  2. 2
    Transfer the file to Device B via email, cloud drive, or USB
  3. 3
    On Device B: Click "Restore Backup (JSON)" and select the file
  4. ✓
    All your data — expenses, goals, income — is restored!
